Process

Identify your Key

Identify your key to see if it is supported. If you dont see it, please send us a photo as we support additional keys which are not listed.

Book an appointment and we will respond within the same business day to arrange technician.

Technician will arrive onsite at the appointment and a new fob copy for your door lock, Condo or Apartment will be made for you right away. Your new copy will be tested to make sure it works.
