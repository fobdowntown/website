Process

Identify your Key Fob

Identify your Key Fob to see if it is supported fob for copy or duplication. If you dont see it, please email or text us a photo as we support additional electronic keys & cards which are not listed.

We don't copy any kinds of remote keys (i.e car keys). We duplicate and copy the proximity chip only of your fob.

Book an Appointment

Book an appointment and we will respond within the same business day to arrange technician.

PRICING: $40 per copy + $30 per additional fob copy

Technician Arrives Onsite

Technician will arrive onsite at the appointment and a new fob copy for your Condo or Apartment will be made for you right away (takes less than 10 minutes). Your new duplicate will be tested to make sure it works.
